About Us

Join us for the most luxurious, yet comfortable salon experience! You can expect the highest quality, cutting-edge service within a beautifully crafted salon atmosphere. Located in best location in town (the middle of everything- Poncey- Highlands & Old 4th Ward), you can enjoy the open space, large architectural windows, & comfortable seating. We offer 2500 sq feet to allow for maximum distancing.

Our caring and talented staff are expertly trained and closely attentive to your needs. Our stylists and management have years of experience working with clients to ensure a great experience, both in the salon & every day till your next visit. The focus is on your needs.
